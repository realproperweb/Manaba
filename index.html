❓ FAQ

EVERYTHING
YOU NEED TO KNOW

How do I register? +
Registration is exclusively via WhatsApp — no forms, no email, no hassle. Simply tap the WhatsApp button anywhere on this page and send us your name and the number of runners. We'll confirm your spot and send all the details!
Who can participate? +
Absolutely everyone! This event is open to all fitness levels — from seasoned runners to first-timers. If you can put one foot in front of the other and enjoy the beach, you're welcome. Walkers are encouraged too!
What is the Mother & Daughter Relay? +
The Mother & Daughter Relay is a special team competition where a mother and daughter pair split the route between them. One starts at Manaba Beach and hands over to the other to finish at Lucien Beach. There are special prizes for the top relay teams — mention "Mother & Daughter Relay" when you WhatsApp us.
What should I bring on race day? +
Bring your running shoes, comfortable gear, water, sunscreen, and a cap — KZN sun is no joke! If you're entering the relay, bring your partner. Most importantly, bring your energy and excitement — it's going to be an amazing day!
Is there a participation fee? +
WhatsApp us to get all the details on entry fees and what's included. We keep it affordable and accessible for the whole community!
Will there be water stations on the route? +
Yes! The route will have water stations and marshals along the way. The event is fully organised for your safety and comfort. First aid support will also be available throughout.
Can I enter on the day? +
We strongly recommend registering in advance as spots are limited and fill up fast. Walk-ins may be accepted if space allows, but don't risk it — WhatsApp us today to secure your spot!
